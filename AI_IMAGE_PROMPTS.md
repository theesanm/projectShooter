## Post-Processing Tips

After generating with AI:
1. **Resize to 64x64** if generated larger
2. **Remove background** if not transparent (use remove.bg or Photoshop)
3. **Enhance edges** for sharper pixel look
4. **Adjust colors** to match specifications:
   - Basic Soldier: #00ff00 (green)
   - Advanced Special Ops: #0088ff (blue)  
   - Elite Commander: #ffaa00 (gold) + #ff0000 (red)
5. **Save as PNG** with transparency enabled

---

## Quick Generation Workflow

1. Copy one of the prompts above
2. Paste into your AI image generator of choice
3. Generate 4-8 variations
4. Download the best result
5. Open in image editor (Photoshop, GIMP, Photopea)
6. Resize to 64x64px (or 128x128px for retina)
7. Ensure background is transparent
8. Export as PNG
9. Save to: `/public/assets/shooters/basic.png`
